About Us

We The People Roleplay is a community-driven FiveM server focused on providing a realistic and engaging roleplay experience. Our server features a diverse job system, custom scripts, and an active community dedicated to immersive storytelling and fun.

Server Info

Our server offers a variety of game modes and unique features, including advanced job systems, custom vehicles, and frequent updates to ensure a seamless roleplay experience. With robust server performance and high uptime, we aim to provide a stable environment for all players.

Server Rules

To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:

  • Respect all players and staff members.
  • No cheating, exploiting, or abusing game mechanics.
  • Follow the roleplay guidelines and maintain in-character behavior.
  • Use common sense and be considerate of others.
  • Report any issues or rule violations to the staff team.
