Server Info

Our server offers a variety of game modes and unique features to ensure an immersive roleplaying experience. Here’s what you can expect:

Game Modes

We provide a range of game modes to cater to different interests and play styles. From law enforcement to emergency services, criminal activities, and civilian roles, there’s something for everyone.

Unique Features

Our server includes numerous custom scripts and mods to enhance gameplay. These features include a sophisticated job system, custom vehicles, and regular updates to keep the experience fresh and exciting.

Server Performance

We prioritize server performance and uptime to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for all players. Our dedicated server infrastructure is designed to handle high player counts and complex scripts without compromising performance.

Community Events

We regularly host community events and activities, offering players the chance to earn rewards and recognition. These events are designed to foster community engagement and provide fun, interactive experiences.
