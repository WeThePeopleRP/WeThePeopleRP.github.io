Rules

To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:

General Rules

  • Respect all players and staff members.
  • No cheating, exploiting, or abusing game mechanics.
  • Follow the roleplay guidelines and maintain in-character behavior.
  • Use common sense and be considerate of others.
  • Report any issues or rule violations to the staff team.

Roleplay Rules

  • Stay in character at all times during roleplay scenarios.
  • No random deathmatching (RDM) or vehicle deathmatching (VDM).
  • Value your life and the lives of others (No Fear RP).
  • Avoid metagaming and powergaming.
  • Use proper roleplay etiquette and communicate effectively with other players.

Server Etiquette

  • Be patient and respectful in all interactions.
  • Follow instructions from staff members promptly and without argument.
  • Refrain from using offensive language or engaging in toxic behavior.
  • Keep the server environment fun and welcoming for everyone.
